Qdoba

Everyone knows that Qdoba is superior to Chipotle and Freebirds. Therefore, you plan to spend every last penny of yours at Qdoba.

Qdoba has a number of options to choose from, and there's no tax! Given that Qdoba has an unlimited supply of food, is it possible for you to choose items on the menu to spend all of your money with no change leftover? You may select multiple items on the menu and the same item multiple times if you wish.

Input

The first line of input will contain a single integer T, the number of test cases. The first line of each test case has two integers, N and M. N is the number of items on Qdoba's menu. M is the amount of money you have. The following N lines each have a menu item price.

Constraints

There are at most 10 test cases. There are at most 1,000 items on Qdoba's menu, and all prices are no greater than $10.00. You can have up to $10,000.

Output

For each test case, print YES if it's possible to spend all of your money without having a single penny leftover; otherwise, print NO.

Sample Input

3
3 5.00
3.00
1.00
0.75
1 4.00
3.99
1 4.00
4.01

Sample Output

YES
NO
NO
